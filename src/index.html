Welcome to the Young African Business School of Manufacturing (YABSOM) – where innovation meets opportunity. We are Africa’s premier training school dedicated to empowering the next generation of manufacturing leaders in Africa. By blending cutting-edge education with practical industry experience, we equip young professionals with the skills, knowledge, and mindset to excel in today’s dynamic manufacturing landscape.
