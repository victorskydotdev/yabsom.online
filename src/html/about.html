About Yabsom

At the Young African Business School of Manufacturing, we are committed to driving Africa’s industrial transformation. Established with a vision to bridge the skills gap in manufacturing, YABSOM focuses on hands-on learning, leadership development, and global competitiveness.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, expert faculty, and strong industry partnerships ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to tackle real-world challenges and contribute to the continent’s growth. Join us as we shape the future of manufacturing in Africa.

Our mission

To inspire and nurture the next generation of African manufacturing professionals by providing world-class education, fostering innovation, and promoting sustainable industrial practices.

Our vision

To be Africa’s leading institution for manufacturing education, creating a generation of leaders who drive economic growth and innovation.
